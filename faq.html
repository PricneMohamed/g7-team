Product Policy & Retrieval Conditions

Frequently asked questions about our product policies and steps for returning or retrieving products.

What is your product return policy?

You can return any product within 14 days if it is unused and still in its original packaging. We'll process your refund promptly.

How do I request a product retrieval?

Submit a request through the “Contact Us” page and include your order number to start the retrieval process. We'll guide you through the next steps.

Are all products eligible for return?

Some products are non-returnable, such as opened personal care items, custom-made products, or perishable goods. Check your order confirmation for details.

How long do I have to request a retrieval?

You must request a retrieval within 14 days of delivery. Requests after this period may not be accepted to ensure product quality.
